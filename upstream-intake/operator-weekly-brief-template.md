## This Period At A Glance

Write this section as a short, natural summary.

It can be a short paragraph, a few bullets, or a small mixed summary. Put unresolved operator decisions near the top when they exist. Routine autonomous decisions can come later.

Useful substance to include when relevant:

- any open operator call
- the one thing worth immediate attention
- anything clearly safe to move ahead on
- compatibility or migration watch items
- security or hardening concerns worth surfacing
